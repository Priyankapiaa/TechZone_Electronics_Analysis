# TechZone Electronics — Sales Performance Analysis

## 🏪 Business Overview
TechZone Electronics is a mid-sized electronics retail chain operating across 5 Indian cities. Despite generating ₹180M in total revenue, the business is experiencing declining profit margins due to heavy discounting, poor inventory management and underperforming city operations.

## ❗ Business Problems
- Which product categories are profitable vs loss-making?
- Are discounts helping sales or killing margins?
- Which cities are underperforming?
- Which products are overstocked or understocked?
- Are there seasonal patterns being missed?

## 🛠️ Tools Used
| Tool | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Google Sheets | Data exploration & pivot analysis |
| SQL Server (SSMS) | Business queries & aggregation |

## 📊 Key Findings
- Computing is the most profitable category — 22.39% margin
- Display category has only 4.74% margin despite ₹37M revenue
- Hyderabad is the only loss-making city — -₹1.5M profit
- 20%+ discounts generate negative margins — 169 loss-making orders
- Monitor & LED TV severely overstocked — 9,852 units
- Smartphones & Smartwatches critically understocked
- Q4 (Oct–Dec) is peak season — festive boost clearly visible

## 📢 Recommendations
- Cap all discounts at 15% maximum
- Investigate Hyderabad pricing and operations
- Clear Display category overstock before reordering
- Urgently restock Smartphones and Smartwatches
- Coach underperforming sales reps on value-based selling
- Launch September promotional campaign
